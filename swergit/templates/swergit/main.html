About
Rajiv Gandhi Institute Of Technology

In 1988, the great Indian visionary leader and philanthropist, late shree Vilasraoji Dagdojiraoji Deshmukh established Manjara charitable trust in Latur for providing quality education to students of Maharashtra. His inspiration and guidance enabled the educational and social institutions under the trust to achieve many recognitions and awards in the past and his social vision will be a motivation for all future initiatives and activities of the institutions under the trust.


The MCT's Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology is an institute affiliated to the University of Mumbai. It offers the Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) undergraduate course which is its flagship course, in five disciplines. Founded in 1992, the institute was inspired by the leadership of Late Mr. Vilasrao Deshmukh. The college is run by the Manjara Charitable Trust and was accredited by the All India Council or Technical Education(AICTE).

RGIT has always been known to be a front runner in organizing various conferences and national level competitions through various interdisciplinary committees. The Institute is playing a key role in providing and promoting the engineering education and research programs across the country

About
Swe Rgit

Society of Women Engineer's is a Global committee which is more than just an organisation that provides movement towards equality and opportunities for Women in Engineering SWE raises awareness of the transformative work engineers to engage in and provide solutions to problems and improve the quality of life.

SWE is recognized for building leadership skills, providing leadership experience, and creating opportunities for exposure to top industrial and educational leaders.


SWE also provides collegiate members valuable access to a unique set of resources that sets the stage for a successful career. The establishment of this committee will provide new roots for aspiring Women and Men Engineers to support the growth and lay a foundation with Equivalent Opportunities for all future Engineer's!

Our Vision

Empower women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders, expand the image of the engineering and technology professions as a positive force in improving the quality of life, and demonstrate the value of diversity and inclusion.
